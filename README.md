Lead Data Engineer at **TOTVS**, driving the transition to a governance-first, data-driven architecture. I design and scale end-to-end data platforms — from ingestion pipelines to semantic layers — and deliver AI integrations that bring LLM-powered capabilities into production data products.

- **6+ years** in data engineering, analytics, and platform architecture
